Kunhimangalam

Kunhimangalam village near Payyannur is famous for its unique bronze lamps and sculptures. The village is situated about 31 km from Kannur town.



Getting there
Nearest railway station: Ezhimala, about 550 m; Payyanur Railway Station, about 9 km
Nearest airport: Kannur International Airport, about 55 km | Calicut International Airport, about 147 km
